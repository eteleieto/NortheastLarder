## Background

We took the [[Oat Koji|oat koji]] and blackened it at 140°F for ten days.

The taste has some bitterness, along with a fruity chocolatiness reminiscent of raw chocolate. Next, we'll be drying it completely and mixing it with fat and sweetener in a wet grinder to see if we can imitate chocolate. Likely, this will need to be part of a blend with other potential substrates.

Rye Amazake Recipe

A naturally sweet, non-alcoholic Japanese drink made by fermenting cooked rye with koji.

#### Instructions

1. **Cook Rye:**  
    Rinse rye thoroughly. Simmer in 4 cups of water for 45–60 minutes until soft but not mushy. Let cool to ~140°F (60°C).
    
2. **Add Koji:**  
    Mix in koji evenly while the rye is still warm.
    
3. **Incubate:**  
    Keep at 130–140°F (55–60°C) for 8–12 hours. Taste for sweetness.
    
4. **Blend (Optional):**  
    Blend for a smoother texture, adding water as needed.
    
5. **Store:**  
    Refrigerate for up to 1 week. Can be served warm or cold.


--- column-break ---

#### Ingredients

- 1 cup whole rye grains
    
- 4 cups water (for cooking)
    
- 100g koji rice (or barley koji)
    
- Additional water for blending (optional)

#### Equipment

- Rice cooker or pot
    
- Thermos or yogurt maker (for incubation)
    
- Blender (optional)

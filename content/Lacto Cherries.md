## Background

This is a simple [[Lacto-Fermentation|lacto-fermentation]] of [[Cherry|cherries]], using a 2% salt brine. A small amount of water was added to ensure the fruit remained submerged.

### Ingredients 

Cherries

Salt (2% of cherry weight)

Water (to cover)

--- column-break ---

### Equipment

Fermentation Jar

Fermentation Weight

--- end-multi-column

## Instructions

1.  **Combine**  
    Add the cherries and salt to a jar.

2.  **Submerge**  
    Add just enough water to cover the cherries, then place a weight on top to keep them submerged.

3.  **Ferment**  
    Seal the jar and allow it to ferment at room temperature.

## Notes and Observations

- The resulting ferment is very tasty and has an almost olive-like quality.
- This raises the question of whether prematurely picked cherries could be cured in a similar manner to [[Olive|olives]].

## Background

This is a naturally fermented soda made from [[Plum|plums]] using a [[Carrot Bug|carrot bug]] as the starter. The process involves blending ripe and slightly underripe plums, diluting the juice, and allowing it to ferment at room temperature to create natural carbonation.

### Ingredients 

A mix of ripe and underripe plums

50ml carrot bug starter

Water

Sugar (optional)

Blender

Strainer

Fermentation Bottle

Cheesecloth

## Instructions

1.  **Juicing**  
    Blend the plums with the skin on and strain the resulting juice.

2.  **Dilution**  
    Dilute the juice with approximately 3.5 parts water.

3.  **Inoculation**  
    Add the carrot bug starter to the diluted juice and bottle it.

4.  **Fermentation**  
    Leave the bottle at room temperature for about 48 hours.

5.  **Chilling**  
    Chill the bottle in the fridge for at least 12 hours to help the CO2 dissolve into the liquid before serving.

## Notes and Observations

- The resulting soda is very tasty, but it could benefit from a slightly longer fermentation time for more fizz.
- Adding a small amount of sugar, beyond what is naturally present in the fruit, could also help increase carbonation.
- For future batches, straining the juice through a cheesecloth would be beneficial to remove as much fine pulp as possible for a clearer result.

## Background

This experiment is an attempt to create a [[Garum|garum]], a type of amino sauce, from what would otherwise be a waste product. This version uses [[Carrot Green|carrot greens]] and [[(WIP) Koji|koji]] to produce a savory, fish sauce-style condiment.

---
## Recipe
--- start-multi-column: ID_2qnt
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

### Ingredients 

360g Carrot Greens

100g Koji

600g Water

118g Salt

--- column-break ---

### Equipment

Fermentation Vessel

Kitchen Scale

--- end-multi-column

## Instructions

1.  **Combine**  
    Add the carrot greens, koji, water, and salt to a fermentation vessel.

2.  **Mix**  
    Stir thoroughly until the salt is dissolved and all ingredients are well-integrated.

3.  **Ferment**  
    Seal the vessel and store at room temperature.

![[Tops Garum.webp|200x267]]

## Notes and Observations

- The koji was sourced from [[Rhapsody Foods]].
- The salt was calculated at 10% of the total weight of the mixture (greens + koji + water + salt).

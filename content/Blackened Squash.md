## Background

This experiment uses [[Buttercup Squash|buttercup squash]], which was peeled and cut into wedges. The goal is to transform the squash using the [[Blackening (Enzymatic)|blackening]] technique, a process similar to making [[Black Garlic|black garlic]].

## Instructions

1.  **Preparation**  
    Peel the buttercup squash and cut it into wedges.

![[Blackened Squash-1.webp|200x267]]

2.  **Bagging**  
    Place the wedges in a vacuum seal bag with a pinch of salt.

3.  **Sealing**  
    Vacuum seal the bag.

4.  **Incubation**  
    Let the bag ferment at 140°F (60°C) for one to two months.

## Notes and Observations

- The process is expected to take at least a month.

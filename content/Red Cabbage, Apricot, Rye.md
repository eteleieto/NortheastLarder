## Background

This dish was developed for a pop-up in June 2025.

## Composition

The dish is composed of several elements:

*   Roasted [[Red Cabbage|red cabbage]]
*   A puree made from roasted [[Lacto-Apricot|lacto-apricot]], [[Apple|apple]], [[Mustard|mustard]], vinegar, and [[Agar|agar]]
*   [[Rye|rye]] breadcrumbs
*   [[Hazelnut|hazelnuts]]
*   Stewed beans

## Notes and Observations

The dish was quite good, but it could use some further refinement.

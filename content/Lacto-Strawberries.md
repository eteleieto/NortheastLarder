## Background

This is an experiment in [[Lacto-Fermentation|lacto-fermentation]], using [[Strawberry|strawberries]] with a 2% salt brine created from their own juices.

Jar

## Instructions

1.  **Preparation**  
    Slice the strawberries in half and place them in a jar with the salt.

2.  **Submerging**  
    Press the strawberries down with a fermentation weight, allowing them to submerge in their own juices as they are released.

3.  **Fermenting**  
    Seal the jar and allow it to ferment at room temperature.

## Notes and Observations

- The taste is sour and distinctly strawberry-like.
- This could be mixed with honey, used in a BBQ-type glaze, or incorporated into desserts.

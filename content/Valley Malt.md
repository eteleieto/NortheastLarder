Valley Malt is the first craft malthouse in New England in over a century, founded by Andrea and Christian Stanley in 2009 in Hadley, Massachusetts. They source 100% Northeast, family farm-grown grain, malting barley, wheat, rye, oats, and more for craft brewers and distillers. Their facility runs on hydroelectric power. We source [[Light Munich Malt]] from them, which we use in garums and amazakes.

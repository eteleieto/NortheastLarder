In historical beer production, sometimes the entire plant was used as a bittering agent (cf. [[Gruit|gruit]]). As part of our experimentation, we've taken all the leaves and done a long cold infusion in water. After a few days of cold infusion, the liquid has taken on some of the taste of chamomile, combined with slight bitterness reminiscent of mugwort.
